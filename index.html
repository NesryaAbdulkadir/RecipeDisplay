  • Spaghetti Carbonara

    Italian

    •

    20 minutes

    •

    2 servings

    Ingredients

    200g spaghetti

    100g pancetta

    2 large eggs

    50g pecorino cheese

    50g parmesan cheese

    Black pepper

    Salt

    Instructions

    1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
    2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
    3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated cheeses.
    4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some pasta water.
    5. Combine spaghetti with pancetta, then remove from heat.
    6. Mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water as needed.
    7. Serve with extra cheese and black pepper.
